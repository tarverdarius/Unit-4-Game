CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

How To Play!

  • The given crystals have random values
  • Click a crystal to reveal the amount for that crystal
  • The object of the game is to match the random number by clicking the combination of Crystals to equal the total of the random number.
  • -you win if you match the number , and you lose if you exceed the number.
  • Whether you win or lose the game will reset and record your losses and wins
  • GOOD LUCK!!
